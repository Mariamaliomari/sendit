Welcome To SendIt

SendIt is a specialist moving company that handles all African moves, packing & unpacking, cleaning, and furniture dismantling & reassembling services.

Are you in search of a removal company that can transport your possessions to a new destination within Kenya or East Africa? Or perhaps you require assistance with packing, unpacking, furniture dismantling, or assembly during your relocation?

Don't hesitate to reach out to SendIt, and we will tailor an affordable price specifically to your requirements. We alleviate the stress associated with moving by offering a comprehensive solution to meet all your relocation needs.

Our aim is to allow you to unwind, savor the moving journey, and settle into your new residence promptly upon arrival. Whether you need assistance with cleaning your current home or require a team to pack your belongings a few days ahead of your move, our experts are here to accommodate your unique needs.

Rest assured, our professionals possess the expertise to transport your possessions from point A to point B in impeccable condition. We specialize in securely packing various items, leaving no room for any potential damage during transit.
