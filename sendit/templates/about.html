About Sendit

Sendit simplifies the process of finding reliable transport services by connecting users with trusted, rated drivers and verified transport companies. Whether you're moving household items, office equipment, or making a small delivery, Sendit helps you compare trusted transport providers, book the right vehicle, and communicate directly with your driver throughout the journey.

Whether you're moving a studio apartment or a whole office, Sendit gives you an instant price, a clear plan, and a direct line of communication with your mover from booking to drop-off.
